# Privacy Policy for Tifinagh Fonts Viewer

**Last Updated: February 2, 2026**

The **Tifinagh Fonts Viewer** is built as an offline-first utility. Your privacy is paramount to us, and our privacy policy is simple and transparent.

## 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal data.** 
- No registration is required.
- No analytics or tracking scripts are used.
- No user-identifiable information is transmitted to our servers or third parties.

## 2. Permissions
The app may require standard permissions necessary for its core functionality:
- **Internet Access**: Only used for external links (e.g., viewing font sources on GitHub) when explicitly triggered by the user.
- **Storage**: No unauthorized storage access is requested. The fonts are bundled within the application.

## 3. Data Storage
All interaction data (such as your chosen theme or custom preview text) is stored locally on your device via shared preferences and never leaves your phone.

## 4. Third-Party Links
The app contains links to GitHub to provide credit to font authors. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of GitHub or any other external sites.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official repository.
